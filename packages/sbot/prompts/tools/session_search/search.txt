Search past conversation history. Use this tool to recall earlier discussions, find specific information mentioned in previous sessions, or locate relevant context from past interactions.

Query format: CNF (Conjunctive Normal Form) — a 2D array of keywords.
- Outer array: AND between groups (all groups must match)
- Inner array: OR within a group (any keyword in the group matches)

Examples:
- [["deployment"]] — messages containing "deployment"
- [["database"], ["migration"]] — messages containing both "database" AND "migration"
- [["error", "fail", "exception"], ["deploy"]] — messages containing ("error" OR "fail" OR "exception") AND "deploy"

Returns matching messages with timestamps and roles, ranked by relevance when supported by the backend, otherwise by recency.
