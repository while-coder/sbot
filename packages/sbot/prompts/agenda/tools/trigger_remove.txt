Remove ONE existing trigger by triggerId from an agenda item; the item itself stays.

USE WHEN
The user drops one reminder/schedule but wants to keep the agenda item: "drop the 18:00 reminder but keep the item".

DO NOT USE WHEN
- Cancelling the whole item → agenda_cancel.
- Clearing all triggers in one shot → agenda_trigger_replace_all with triggers=[].

The trigger is disabled, not deleted; history is preserved.
