Mark an agenda item (or one occurrence of a routine) as complete.

NON-OCCURRENCE ITEMS (todo / reminder / routine without per-fire check-in)
Use this when the user says it's done: "已经做完了", "搞定了", "已交". The whole item is closed and remaining triggers are disabled.

OCCURRENCE ROUTINES (completionMode=occurrence)
Closes ONE instance, NOT the whole routine. The routine keeps firing.
- Without `at`: closes the EARLIEST still-pending instance — for plain check-ins like "我喝了 / 完成了今天的 / 已交".
- With `at`: pinpoint a specific occurrence by ISO datetime. BOTH pending AND missed instances are reachable through `at`. Use it for:
  * "16:38 喝了水忘了说" → at = 16:38 ISO of today (likely closes the missed 16:38 instance)
  * "周一的周报我刚补交了" → at = that monday's scheduled fire ISO
  * "10 点的工作报告：…" against an hourly routine → at = 10:00 ISO of today
- Do NOT use `at` for ambiguous "现在 / just now / 刚刚" replies — let the default earliest-pending logic handle them.

To stop the whole routine entirely, use agenda_cancel instead.
